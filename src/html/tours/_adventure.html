Adventure Tours

Ready for an adrenaline-fueled adventure? Our adventure tours offer you the chance to conquer high peaks, raft down wild rivers, explore the depths of ancient jungles, and feel the spirit of freedom in the most remote corners of Peru

Amazon Rainforest Expedition

Embark on an unforgettable adventure into the heart of the Amazon rainforest, one of the most biodiverse regions on Earth. Trek through lush jungle trails, navigate winding rivers by canoe, and encounter a dazzling array of wildlife, from colorful birds to elusive jaguars. Immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of this pristine wilderness as you explore its hidden wonders.

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Highlights: Guided jungle treks, wildlife spotting, canopy walkway experience, nocturnal jungle exploration.
  • Accommodation: Rustic eco-lodges deep in the Amazon rainforest.
  • Price: Starting from $1200 per person

Andean Adventure: Trek to Rainbow Mountain

Embark on a thrilling trek to Rainbow Mountain, a geological wonder known for its stunning natural beauty and vibrant colors. Hike through breathtaking landscapes of towering peaks, turquoise lakes, and Andean valleys, culminating in the awe-inspiring sight of the multicolored mountain slopes. Immerse yourself in the rugged beauty of the Andes as you conquer this iconic trek.

  • Duration: 2 days
  • Highlights: Trek to Rainbow Mountain, explore Red Valley, encounter local Quechua communities, witness Andean wildlife.
  • Accommodation: Cozy mountain lodges with panoramic views of the Andes.
  • Price: Starting from $500 per person
