Journey through the clouds to the legendary Machu Picchu, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and marvel at the ancient Inca citadel shrouded in mist. Explore the mysteries of its ruins, hike along scenic trails, and immerse yourself in the rich history and spirituality of this iconic desti nation.

Sacred Valley Cultural Encounter

Embark on a journey through the Sacred Valley of the Incas, a land of breathtaking beauty and profound spiritual significance. Explore ancient Inca ruins, traditional Andean villages, and bustling markets. Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and traditions of the Quechua people as you discover the heart of the Inca Empire.

  • Duration: 2 days
  • Highlights: Visit Pisac market and ruins, explore Ollanta Tambo fortress, interact with local communities, enjoy traditional Peruvian cuisine.
  • Accommodation: Charming guesthouses overlooking the Sacred Valley.
  • Price: Starting from $600 per person
