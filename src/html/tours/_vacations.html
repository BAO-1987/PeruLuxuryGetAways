Beach Vacations

Beachside relaxation along the shores of the Pacific Ocean in Peru offers a unique blend of serene landscapes, vibrant culture, and exquisite cuisine. Explore our beach vacation options for the ultimate coastal getaway

Tranquil Retreat in Mancora

Escape to the idyllic shores of Mancora, where pristine beaches meet crystal-clear waters. Indulge in days of relaxation as you soak up the sun, unwind in hammocks swaying gently in the ocean breeze, and savor fresh seafood delicacies. Immerse yourself in the laid-back atmosphere of this charming beach town and let your worries drift away.

  • Duration: 4 days
  • Highlights: Relax on Mancora Beach, indulge in seafood feasts, enjoy water sports like surfing and kiteboarding, explore nearby Punta Sal.
  • Accommodation: Beachfront bungalows with private terraces overlooking the Pacific Ocean.
  • Price: Starting from $700 per person

Adventurous Beach Getaway in Paracas

Combine beach relaxation with thrilling adventures in Paracas, a coastal paradise teeming with natural wonders. Explore the otherworldly landscapes of the Paracas National Reserve, home to dramatic cliffs, red sand beaches, and diverse marine life. Dive into exhilarating water sports, embark on wildlife watching excursions, and discover the beauty of Peru's Pacific coast.

  • Duration: 3 days
  • Highlights: Boat tour to the Ballestas Islands, explore Paracas National Reserve, sandboarding on the dunes, relax on Paracas Beach.
  • Accommodation: Stylish beachfront hotels with panoramic views of the ocean and easy access to the Reserve.
  • Price: Starting from $600 per person
