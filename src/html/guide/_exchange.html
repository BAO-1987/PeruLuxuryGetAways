Currency and Exchange

  • Peruvian Sol (PEN): The official currency.
  • Currency Exchange: Easily accessible at banks, exchange offices, and hotels.
  • ATMs: Widely available; be mindful of withdrawal fees.
  • Managing Finances: Carry a blend of cash and cards for convenience.
  • Tips: Check exchange rates, withdraw larger amounts, and use cards with no foreign transaction fees.
