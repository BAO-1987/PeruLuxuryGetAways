Transportation

Explore Peru effortlessly with diverse options: domestic flights for city hops, buses linking major destinations, and scenic train routes like the Andean Explorer. Consider renting a car cautiously. Navigate cities via buses and taxis, prioritizing safety and planning regional travel ahead, especially during busy times, for a seamless Peruvian experience

Packing List

  • Coastal: Mild year-round. Summers (Dec-Mar) are warmer. Rain is rare.
  • Highlands: Dry (May-Sep) for trekking. Rainy (Oct-Apr) for lush landscapes.
  • Amazon: Wet (Dec-Mar) with heavy rainfall. Dry (Apr-Nov) for better wildlife viewing.

Best Times to Visit:

  • Coastal: Summer for beach activities.
  • Highlands: Dry season for trekking.
  • Amazon: Dry season for wildlife viewing.

Tips:

  • Pack according to region.
  • Check forecasts and prepare for changes.
