Weather and Climate

Planning your Peru trip? Here's a quick overview

Climate Overview:

  • Coastal: Mild year-round. Summers (Dec-Mar) are warmer. Rain is rare.
  • Highlands: Dry (May-Sep) for trekking. Rainy (Oct-Apr) for lush landscapes.
  • Amazon: Wet (Dec-Mar) with heavy rainfall. Dry (Apr-Nov) for better wildlife viewing.

Best Times to Visit:

  • Coastal: Summer for beach activities.
  • Highlands: Dry season for trekking.
  • Amazon: Dry season for wildlife viewing.

Tips:

  • Pack according to region.
  • Check forecasts and prepare for changes.

Enjoy exploring Peru's diverse climates and landscapes!
