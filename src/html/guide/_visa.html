Visa and Entry Requirements

Planning your trip to Peru? Here's what you need to know about visa requirements and entry documents

  • Visa Types

    Tourists from many countries enjoy visa-free stays up to 90 days. Longer stays or specific activities may require a visa

  • Entry Documents

    All visitors to Peru must have a valid passport with at least six months' validity from the date of entry

  • Specific Requirements

    Check your nationality's specific visa requirements. Additional documents like proof of onward travel or health certificates may be necessary
