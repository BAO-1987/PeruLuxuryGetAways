• Local Customs and Etiquette

    Respect Peru's cultural norms by using titles like "Señor" or "Señora" when greeting, dressing modestly, and showing respect for elders and authority figures. Be mindful of personal space, adapt to local punctuality norms, and learn basic Spanish phrases for communication. Embrace Peru's rich culture with understanding and respect

  • Health and Safety

    Stay safe and healthy in Peru by getting necessary vaccinations, practicing food and water safety, acclimating gradually to high altitudes, and being cautious of pickpocketing and crime. Stay informed about natural disaster risks, register with your embassy, have travel insurance, and keep emergency contacts handy. Enjoy your trip safely!
